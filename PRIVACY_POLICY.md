# Kirik Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 2025**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address** (if you register with email/password or Google/Apple)
- **Display name / username** you choose
- **Profile photo** (optional, if you upload one)

### Gameplay Data
- **Game history** (scores, words drawn, win rate)
- **In-game chat messages** (stored temporarily for game session)
- **Reports** submitted against other players

### Technical Data
- **Device type and OS version** (for crash reporting)
- **IP address** (for rate limiting and fraud prevention, not stored long-term)
- **Firebase Analytics events** (disabled by default, opt-in only)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To operate and improve the game
- To maintain leaderboards and player statistics
- To enforce our community rules and process reports
- To provide customer support

We **never sell** your personal data to third parties.

---

## 3. Data Storage

- Account data is stored securely in **Firebase Firestore** (Google Cloud)
- Game sessions are stored temporarily in **Redis** (cleared after 2 hours of inactivity)
- Firebase's security infrastructure protects all stored data

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Kirik is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us.

---

## 6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data by:
1. Going to **Profile → Settings → Delete Account**
2. Emailing us at: privacy@yourstudio.com

We will permanently delete all your data within 30 days.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
