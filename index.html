FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I create a room?

Tap "Create Room" on the main menu, choose your settings (players, rounds, language, difficulty) and tap Create. Share the room code with friends.

How do I join a friend's game?

Tap "Join Room" and enter the 6-character room code. You can also tap a kirik.me/join/CODE link to open the game directly.

The app shows "Connection failed" when creating a room

Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, close the app completely, reopen it, and try again from the main menu.

Voice chat isn't working

Make sure you've granted microphone permission to Kirik in your device Settings app. Then tap the Voice button in-game to activate it.

How do I report a player?

Tap the flag icon next to a player's name during the game to submit a report. Our team reviews all reports.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. You can also email us at support@kirik.me.

Is Kirik free?

Yes — Kirik is completely free to play with no ads. All features including voice chat, leaderboards, and 5 languages are free.
