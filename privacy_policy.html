Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address, chosen username, and profile photo (optional). Guest players only require a nickname.

Gameplay Data: We store game history including scores, words drawn, and win statistics to power leaderboards and your profile.

Technical Data: Device type and OS version for crash reporting. IP addresses for rate limiting (not stored long-term).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Operate the Kirik game and maintain your account
  • Display scores on global and friends leaderboards
  • Process reports of rule violations
  • Improve the game experience

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

All account data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Frankfurt, EU servers). Game sessions are cached temporarily in Redis and automatically cleared after 2 hours of inactivity. We use TLS/SSL encryption for all data transmission.

5. Children's Privacy

Kirik is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately for deletion.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account in the app, or contact us by email.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings. We will permanently delete all your data within 30 days of your request.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you of significant changes via the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
